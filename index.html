Key Phase Observations
Mar 2025 — Campaign Start
Enrolment begins at low baseline (~5–10K/day); initial ramp-up phase as enrolment centres activate across states.
Late Mar – Apr 2025 — First Surge
Sharp jump to ~255K; likely linked to campaign launch in large-population states like Uttar Pradesh and Bihar.
Jun – Jul 2025 — Peak Activity
Highest single-day enrolment recorded (~615K) in early July; coincides with pre-monsoon outreach drives and summer school campaigns.
Aug – Sep 2025 — Sharp Decline
Rapid fall to sub-100K levels; post-campaign normalisation and monsoon-related logistics disruption in field operations.
Oct 2025 – Jan 2026 — Steady State
Stabilisation at 50–150K/day with occasional spikes; consistent ongoing enrolment supported by localised district-level drives.
